Pelageya (Пелагея)

Born in Novosibirsk, Rusia, the singer Pelageya Sergeyevna Khanova (n. 1986) is an artist who puts into rock and folk-rock arrangements traditional Russian songs, from the folklore or historical, such as cossacks' songs.
