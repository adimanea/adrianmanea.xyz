Fermat's Library

The Fermat's Library project started by promoting some scientific articles which were highly influential throughout history, from mathematics, physics, computer science, chemistry and biology. They republished articles of P. Dirac, R. Feynman, F. Dyson, J. M. Keynes, E. Dijkstra, A. Turing and others in a format that is reminiscent of the marginal notes that P. Fermat used to take on books. As such, the articles are available for reading on-site, and the users can highlight or write notes on them. The notes are visible for the other readers and as such they create a social content, ripe for discussions and debates starting from the articles.

One can also subscribe to their newsletter, to receive periodically the article of the week or of the month, via email.
